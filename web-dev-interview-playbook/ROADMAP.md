## Daily Routine (Recommended)

```
30 min  → Read 5–8 interview questions (answer OUT LOUD)
30 min  → Code 1 small challenge or polyfill
30 min  → Project Bible — 10 questions about YOUR project
30 min  → Revision sheet for today's topic
30 min  → Mock or self-record one answer
```

## Priority Order (If Short on Time)

1. **Project Interview Bible** — most student interviews spend 40%+ on projects
2. **JavaScript Core** — appears in almost every round
3. **React** — if role is frontend/full-stack
4. **Node/Express** — if role is backend/full-stack
5. **HR section** — often underestimated, frequently eliminates candidates

## Tracking Progress

Create a simple checklist:

- [ ] Can explain project in 2 min, 5 min, and 15 min versions
- [ ] Completed 3 mock interviews with feedback
- [ ] Revised all `revision-sheet.md` files once
- [ ] Can answer top 20 "why" questions from `11-interview-patterns/`
- [ ] Written answers to 10 HR behavioral questions

Good luck. Consistency beats cramming.
